Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, tablet, or mobile phone by websites that you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide reporting information.

Cookies can be classified into:

  • **Session Cookies:** These are temporary and are deleted once you close your browser.
  • **Persistent Cookies:** These remain on your device for a set period or until you manually delete them.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for two main purposes: **Functionality** and **Monetization/Analytics**.

A. Essential (Strictly Necessary) Cookies

These cookies are required for the website to function properly and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you, such as logging in or setting your privacy preferences. Examples include storing a session ID to keep you logged in to your user or business dashboard and preventing fraudulent activity.

B. Performance and Functionality Cookies (First-Party)

These cookies allow us to remember the choices you make (such as your preferred city or category filter) and provide enhanced, more personalized features. They also help us track how the site is performing. They are used to track the number of visitors, which pages are most popular, and how users move around the site (using tools like Google Analytics).

C. Targeting and Advertising Cookies (Third-Party)

These cookies are set by third-party advertising partners and are crucial for our revenue model.

  • **Ad Delivery & Relevance:** They track your browsing habits across different websites to serve you advertisements that are most relevant to your interests.
  • **Frequency Capping:** Ensures you don't see the same advertisement too many times.
  • **Affiliate Tracking:** Tracks if you were redirected to a partner site (like a bank or mutual fund AMC) from InvestSherpa.in to ensure we receive the correct commission.

3. Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies.

Browser Controls

Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer.

  • **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and Security > Site Settings > Cookies and site data.
  • **Firefox:** Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data.
  • **Edge/Internet Explorer:** Settings > Privacy, search, and services.
If you choose to decline cookies, some features of our Service may not function correctly, particularly login access and saving your search filters.

Advertising Networks

You can opt-out of interest-based advertising from Google and other ad networks. You can manage your preferences for the advertisements Google shows you by visiting the Google Ad Settings page. Organizations like the Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA) / Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) provide tools that allow you to opt-out of many third-party ad networks in one place.

Cookie Consent Banner

Upon your first visit to InvestSherpa.in, you will see a Cookie Consent Banner giving you the option to **Accept** or **Manage Preferences** for our use of non-essential cookies.
